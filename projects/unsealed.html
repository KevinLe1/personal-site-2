unSEAled

unSEAled is the Southeast Asian zine of Columbia University, and serves as a publication to elevate Southeast Asian and Southeast Asian American voices and art. This is also the first collaboration to exist across every Southeast Asian group at Columbia. I founded this project while I was president of Columbia's Vietnamese Students Association, and it has since become annual.

This project means a lot to me as I grew up seeing little Southeast Asian representation. I am really happy with how unSEAled has grown and hope that it continues to push the boundaries of Southeast Asian and Southeast Asian American representation.
