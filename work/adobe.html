Enterprise Architecture Analyst

As an enterprise architecture analyst within Adobe's Digital Strategy & Solutions (DSS) Group, I support engagements with different businesses to develop strategies and technology solutions that enable customer-experience transformation by leveraging the Adobe Experience Cloud. This typically involves (1) understanding an enterprise's business objectives, pain points, use cases, and current tech stack and (2) collaborating with the respective enterprise to roadmap to a future-state technology architecture.

Additionally, I am responsible for supporting the operations of the DSS Enterprise Architecture team and own the reporting process. I also developed an internal web application for automating early documentation processes to speed up engagements.
